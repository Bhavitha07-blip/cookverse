Soft Idli

Ingredients: Parboiled Rice, Urad Dal, Fenugreek seeds, Salt.

Process:
1. Soak rice and dal separately for 6 hours.
2. Grind into a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
3. Grease plates, pour batter, and steam for 10-12 minutes.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients: Dosa batter, Boiled Potatoes, Onion, Turmeric, Ghee.

Process:
1. Sauté onions and mashed potatoes with turmeric for filling.
2. Spread batter thin on a hot tawa.
3. Cook until crisp, add filling, and fold.

Medu Vada

Ingredients: Urad Dal, Peppercorns, Ginger, Curry leaves.

Process:
1. Grind soaked dal into a thick, fluffy paste.
2. Mix in spices.
3. Shape into doughnuts and deep fry until golden brown.

Rava Upma

Ingredients: Semolina (Rava), Water, Onion, Mustard seeds, Ginger.

Process:
1. Roast rava until fragrant.
2. Sauté spices and onions.
3. Add water and salt; bring to a boil.
4. Stir in rava and cook until thickened.

Ven Pongal

Ingredients: Rice, Moong Dal, Ghee, Black pepper, Cumin, Cashews.

Process:
1. Pressure cook rice and dal together until very soft.
2. Temper cumin, pepper, and cashews in ghee.
3. Mix tempering into the mashed rice-dal mix.

Hotel Sambar

Ingredients: Toor Dal, Mixed Veggies, Tamarind, Sambar powder.

Process:
1. Cook dal until soft.
2. Boil veggies in tamarind water with spice powder.
3. Add cooked dal, simmer, and temper with mustard.

Tomato Rasam

Ingredients: Tomatoes, Tamarind water, Rasam powder, Garlic, Ghee.

Process:
1. Mash tomatoes into tamarind water.
2. Add rasam powder and simmer until frothy.
3. Temper with garlic and mustard in ghee.

Lemon Rice

Ingredients: Cooked Rice, Lemon juice, Peanuts, Turmeric, Green chilies.

Process:
1. Prepare a tempering of mustard, peanuts, and turmeric.
2. Turn off heat and add lemon juice.
3. Mix gently into cooled, cooked rice.

Curd Rice

Ingredients: Overcooked Rice, Fresh Curd, Milk, Ginger, Curry leaves.

Process:
1. Mash rice and mix with milk and salt.
2. Once cool, mix in curd.
3. Temper with mustard and ginger; garnish as desired.

Bisi Bele Bath

Ingredients: Rice, Toor Dal, Veggies, Tamarind, Bisi Bele Bath powder.

Process:
1. Cook rice, dal, and veggies together.
2. Add spice powder and tamarind extract.
3. Simmer until thick; finish with ghee and cashews.

Semiya Payasam

Ingredients: Vermicelli, Milk, Sugar, Cardamom, Ghee-fried nuts.

Process:
1. Roast semiya in ghee until golden.
2. Cook roasted semiya in boiling milk until soft.
3. Add sugar and cardamom; simmer for 5 minutes.

Unniyappam

Ingredients: Rice flour, Jaggery syrup, Ripe Bananas, Coconut bits.

Process:
1. Mix mashed bananas, rice flour, and jaggery into a thick batter.
2. Add coconut bits.
3. Fry in an 'Appe' pan with ghee until dark brown.

Rava Kesari

Ingredients: Rava, Sugar, Ghee, Cashews, Saffron/Food color.

Process:
1. Roast rava in ghee.
2. Cook rava in boiling water until soft.
3. Stir in sugar and color; cook until it leaves the pan sides.

Pesarattu

Ingredients: Whole Green Gram, Ginger, Green chilies, Salt.

Process:
1. Soak moong dal for 5 hours.
2. Grind into a smooth batter with ginger/chilies.
3. Spread thin on tawa and cook until crispy golden.

Onion Uttapam

Ingredients: Dosa batter, Finely chopped Onions, Green chilies, Oil.

Process:
1. Pour thick batter on tawa (don't spread).
2. Top with onions and chilies.
3. Flip and cook on both sides until onions caramelize.

Mangalore Buns

Ingredients: 2 Ripe Bananas, 2 cups Maida, 1/2 cup Curd, Sugar, Cumin, Oil.

Process:
1. Mash bananas with sugar and cumin.
2. Mix with flour and curd to form a soft dough; ferment for 8 hours.
3. Roll into thick puris and deep fry until they puff up and turn golden brown.
